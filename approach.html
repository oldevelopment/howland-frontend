Howland agreement

Howland manages personalized investment strategies. Each portfolio reflects a family or individual’s unique circumstances, time horizon, risk acceptance, and other holdings. We further modify portfolio models to help families and managers frame how money may be invested and how to position performance around the needs of families, values of individuals, and the stages of life.

The personal information Howland and Associates, LLC obtains about our customers is an important responsibility. Security and privacy are paramount to the integrity of our practice. The privacy policy at Howland and Associates, LLC is optimized to ensure that the investment services are accurate and provide superior customer care. The firm collects limited personal information in order to manage portfolios and client relationships. We maintain limited physical and electronic records.

In order to preserve our clients’ right to confidentiality and our proprietary investment strategy, Howland and Associates, LLC does not sell data. Nonpublic information may be shared with third party organizations or companies solely to service accounts and with others who have specifically been authorized by the client. If it is necessary for us to disclose any information in a way not specified above, the client will be given advance notice and have an opportunity to decline such disclosure. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact Rob Howland at rob@investhowland.com.

Earnings Season

Earnings season is when companies announce the profitability for the most recent quarter. The release of new earnings data is a critical time for Howland to make decisions on whether to buy or sell.
