Portfolio models

Howland manages personalized investment strategies. Each portfolio reflects a family or individual’s unique circumstances, time horizon, risk acceptance, and other holdings. We further modify portfolio models to help families and managers frame how money may be invested and how to position performance around the needs of families, values of individuals, and the stages of life.

Opportunistic Growth

The Opportunistic Growth strategy model is for disciplined investors looking for our best long-term performance.

Core

The Core Family strategy portfolio model is a strategy and model that allows the most flexibility with a client’s specific and customized situation.

Triton

The Triton strategy model is a diversified portfolio with companies that, in some capacity, improve water quality issues or reduce pollution. This model invests in companies that take the initiative.
